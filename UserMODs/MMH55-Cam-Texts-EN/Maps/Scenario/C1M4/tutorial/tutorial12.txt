A hero can belong to a certain class which grants him access to special attributes and skills. Clases are divided into three categories:
- Might - improve army cappability, directly aid combat warfare and frequenty take direct action on the battlefield.
- Magic - favour the use of magic manipulation in various ways that destroy enemies or reinforce allies.
- Balanced - jack of all trades with access to a mix of skills both Might and Magic. Being no masters of any they make up for with unique skill combinations that lead them to success.
